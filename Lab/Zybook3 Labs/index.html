Charlie Puth Fan Webpage

Charles Otto Puth Jr. (born December 2, 1991) is an American singer, songwriter, and record producer.
His initial exposure came through the viral success of his song videos uploaded to YouTube.
Puth signed with the record label eleveneleven in 2011 after performing on The Ellen DeGeneres Show, while songwriting and producing material for other artists.
